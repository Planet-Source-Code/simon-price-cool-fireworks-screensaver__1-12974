About

This is a small program I have made in VB, and it's intended use is as a screensaver. It uses DirectX 7 although it also works on DirectX 8 (I've tested it on DX8) and really shows the power of DirectX. It features a display of 20 simultaneous fireworks, all whooshing, exploding, then blending and blurring into the night sky! It runs very smooth on my PC, if you find it runs a bit slow then you can change the first line of code, change the constant NUM_FIREWORKS to a lower number and the program will run faster (although with less fireworks).

Requirements

  • DirectX 7.0 or higher installed

  • VB6 (or MSVB60.DLL for the exe)

  • A monitor capable of 640 x 480 resolution 24 bit color
